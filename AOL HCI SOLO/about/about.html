About Us


VhoTel is a legendary hotel in Jakarta, offering impeccable service since its establishment in 1999. We pride ourselves on providing an exceptional experience for our guests, ensuring their comfort and satisfaction throughout their stay.


Our hotel boasts a diverse selection of rooms, catering to different preferences and group sizes. From our cozy 2-bed rooms to our spacious 1-bed rooms, we have thoughtfully designed accommodations to suit various needs. For those seeking the epitome of luxury, our extravagant suite offers three bedrooms and a fully equipped kitchen, providing a truly indulgent experience.


At VhoTel, we understand the importance of relaxation and wellness. That's why we offer an array of facilities to enhance our guests' well-being. Our spa facilities are designed to rejuvenate the mind and body, providing a serene sanctuary to escape the hustle and bustle of the city. For fitness enthusiasts, our well-equipped gym allows guests to maintain their exercise routines during their stay. And when it's time to unwind, our inviting pools provide the perfect setting to take a refreshing dip or simply bask in the sun.


Throughout our history, we have remained committed to delivering exceptional service and creating memorable experiences for our guests. Our dedicated team of professionals is here to ensure every aspect of your stay exceeds your expectations.


Choose VhoTel for an unforgettable hotel experience in Jakarta. Discover the perfect blend of comfort, luxury, and impeccable service that has made us a legendary destination since 1999.
